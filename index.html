This is our vacation in Italy

  • Day 1 - Verona

    • We visited The house Of Juliet and went up to her famouse balcony. We took a picture with the Statue of Juliet - which according to the local tradition touching her right breast brings you luck.

  • Day 2 - Venice

    • Known as one of the most beautiful cities in the world.Its famouse canals make up a large network in the form of the streets that starts from the Grand Canal.Unfortunately, we couldn't make it to the gondolas.

  • Day 3 - Dolmite Mountains

    • A breath taken mountain range where you can hike, bike and ski.

  • Day 4 - Gardaland Park

    • On the forth day we enjoyed the amusement park where we went on roller coasters, water rides, family rides and many more fun atractions.

  • Day 5 - Verona Waterfalls

    • A spectacular waterfall flowing through a cave, surrounded by beautiful gardens and nature view. Also provides a great observation view over Lake Garda.

  • Day 6 - Movieland

    • Movieland Park is the first Italian amusement park on cinema dedicated to the family with 100,000 square meters of emotions, attractions, shows and more.

  • Day 7 - Lake Garda

    • The largest lake in Italy and a popular destination for a vacation.
